Our Services

  • Elite Investments is a national service provider specialized in integrated healthcare and hazardous waste management through the most effective and market-tailored solutions that meet both national and international standards.

Medical Waste

Elite’s medical waste management program is designed to provide our customers with comprehensive, flexible, consistent, and safe solutions that are compliant with local, state/provincial, and national regulations. Our reliable and cost-effective services consist of:

  • On-call or scheduled collections
  • Safe Transportation.
  • Safe disposal of healthcare waste
  • Regulated Medical Waste packaging Supplies and consumables delivery (e.g., red bags, sharps containers, boxes, etc.)
  • Capacity building and awareness campaigns

Hazardous Waste

We are a nationwide company providing hazardous waste disposal services. Our team of hazardous waste disposal specialists, coupled with a network of partners, make us among the leading hazardous waste collection and disposal service providers. In addition to hazardous waste disposal services, we offer solutions for chemical waste, toxic waste, and all fluid waste disposal.
